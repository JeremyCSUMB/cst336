The Macintosh operating system originated in the year 1984, alongside the release of the Apple Macintosh.

It is an operating system designed for personal computers

MacOS is primarily coded in C++, C, and Swift

Originally marketed as an easy to use operating system, MacOS still is known as a very intuitive operating system.

Many programmers prefer MacOS because of its ease of use, versitility, and reliability.

Quick facts about MacOS

  • Certain aspects of MacOS was shared with Windows for a brief period of time in the 1990's.
  • Since then MacOS has never been legitimately obtained on any non Apple devices.
  • MacOS runs on a UNIX kernal.
